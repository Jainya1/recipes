Cabbage Roll Casserole

Description of the Recipe

Cabbage rolls are a culinary standard in Montenegro, Bosnia and Herzegovina, Turkey, Bulgaria, Macedonia, Romania, Serbia and Croatia

Leaves of brined cabbage stuffed with ground beef, pork and rice are a favorite dish during Christmas time and other non-fasting holidays, while meat is omitted or substituted with crushed walnuts on fasting holy days. Traditionally, cabbage rolls are simmered at length in a paprika-based sauce with chunks of smoked bacon.
